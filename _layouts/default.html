This dashboard displays retailing and distribution locations and related information of the Western New York (WNY) food system.  


Food retailing and distribution are related to the selling of food to consumers to be prepared or eaten off-site. Food retail takes place in a variety of venues, including grocery stores, farmers' markets, general merchandise stores, and corner stores. Consumers include households, individuals, and institutions.


Data sources:

  • New York State Department of Agriculture and Markets
  • USDA Agricultural Marketing Service
  • U.S. Census Bureau
  • U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis
